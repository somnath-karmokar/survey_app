Participants understand that completing the survey does not guarantee any rewards or incentives.

Participation in the cash draw is open to individuals who meet the eligibility criteria outlined in the draw rules.

By entering the cash draw, participants agree to abide by the rules and decisions of the draw administrators.

By participating in the SuDraw survey, you agree to provide accurate and truthful responses to all survey questions. Participants must be of legal age or have parental/guardian consent to participate in the survey.

Only one account is to be created by a user, a mechanism is in place to detect a user creating a second or multiple account. Such participants will be suspended.

Cash draw participants understand that winners will be selected randomly from eligible entries and agree to accept the outcome of the draw.

To participate in surveys, you need to create an account, log in, and browse available surveys. Select a survey that interests you and complete all questions to submit your response.

Winners are selected randomly from participants who have completed the required number of surveys. The selection process is transparent and fair, using a random number generator to ensure equal chances for all eligible participants.

Prizes include various gift vouchers such as Amazon Vouchers, Flipkart Vouchers, and BookMyShow Vouchers. The specific prize amounts may vary depending on the current promotion.
